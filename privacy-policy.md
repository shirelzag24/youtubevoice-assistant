# Privacy Policy – YouTube Voice Assistant

**Last updated:** July 17, 2026

## Overview
YouTube Voice Assistant is a Chrome extension that enables voice control of YouTube videos. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect any personal information
- We do not store your voice recordings
- We do not track your browsing history
- We do not share any data with third parties

## Microphone Access
The extension uses your microphone solely to recognize voice commands (such as "pause", "play", "rewind"). Audio is processed locally by the browser's built-in Web Speech API and is never sent to our servers.

## Anonymous Analytics
We collect anonymous usage events (such as "extension installed" or "YouTube watch page opened") via Umami Analytics. No personal data or identifiable information is collected.
